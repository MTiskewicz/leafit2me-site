Why It Matters

The Cost of Neglecting Your Gutters

🏠 Foundation Damage

Clogged gutters overflow and pool water against your foundation. Over time this causes cracking, settling, and expensive structural repairs.

🧊 Ice Dams

In Wisconsin winters, blocked gutters trap water that freezes and expands under your shingles — causing roof leaks and fascia damage.

🪲 Pest Nesting

Leaves and debris in gutters are prime nesting material for mosquitoes, birds, squirrels, and carpenter ants looking for a way in.

🪵 Fascia & Soffit Rot

Standing water in sagging gutters spills over and soaks the wood fascia boards, causing rot that spreads to the soffit and beyond.
