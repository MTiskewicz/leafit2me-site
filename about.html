About Leaf It 2 Me

A locally owned gutter cleaning company you can trust — based in West Allis and serving Milwaukee homeowners since day one.

Our Story

Milwaukee's Trusted Gutter Experts

Leaf It 2 Me was built on a simple idea: homeowners deserve a gutter service that actually shows up, does the job right, and communicates clearly. Based in West Allis, WI, we serve Milwaukee and Waukesha Counties with the kind of attention to detail you'd expect from a neighbor, not a call center.

Every job is handled personally. You won't deal with a dispatcher or a rotating crew of strangers — you'll work with someone who takes pride in their work and wants to earn your long-term trust.

We're Leafblaster Pro Certified & Raindrop Pro Certified and fully licensed and insured, meaning we meet the highest professional standards in the gutter industry. We carry both Leafblaster Pro and Raindrop Pro gutter guards and recommend the best fit for your home — or tell you honestly if guards aren't the right call.

Our Commitment

Why Homeowners Choose Us

🏠 Locally Owned & Operated

We live and work in the Milwaukee area. When you hire us, your money stays in the community and you get a provider who understands local conditions — the oak trees, the clay soils, the Wisconsin winters.

📸 Full Transparency

Every job comes with before and after photos sent directly to your inbox, plus a written condition report documenting any issues we find. No surprises. No guessing whether the work was done.

🔒 Licensed & Insured

We carry full liability insurance and are properly licensed to work in Wisconsin. You're protected in the unlikely event something goes wrong — we wouldn't have it any other way.

✅ Leafblaster Pro Certified & Raindrop Pro Certified

We're certified to install Leafblaster Pro and we also carry Raindrop Pro — two of the best guard systems on the market. We'll recommend the right one for your home, or be straight with you if guards aren't a fit.
